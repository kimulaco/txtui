## Basic

```
Alice 10:00
+---------------------------+
| Hi! How are you?          |
+---------------------------+

                        Bob 10:01
    +---------------------------+
    | I'm good, thanks!         |
    +---------------------------+

Alice 10:02
+---------------------------+
| Great to hear!            |
+---------------------------+
```

## With System Message

```
   --- Alice joined the chat ---

Alice 10:00
+---------------------------+
| Hello everyone!           |
+---------------------------+

                        Bob 10:01
    +---------------------------+
    | Welcome!                  |
    +---------------------------+

    --- Jan 2, 2025 ---

Alice 09:00
+---------------------------+
| Good morning!             |
+---------------------------+
```
